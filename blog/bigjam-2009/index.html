    BIGJam 2009

    By Donald Hutchison August 10, 2009 Comment
    >
    photo by sir alex may

    Berlin was incredible. I made 3 terrible games, each in their own 3 hour segments. I also met some very cool people and learnt a lot about games dev.

    Most importantly, I think I've unstuck all that was keeping me back and blocking my ideas. I did so much more in one of those 3 hour periods than I've probably done all summer. Can't wait for next year to roll around.
